Popular Skill Guides

  • Attack: Increases melee accuracy to land more hits.
  • Strength: Boosts melee damage output.
  • Defence: Reduces incoming melee, ranged, and magic damage.
  • Hitpoints: Determines your maximum health.
  • Ranged: Boosts accuracy and damage with bows and crossbows.
  • Prayer: Unlocks defensive, offensive, and utility prayers.
  • Magic: Cast spells for combat, teleportation, and alchemy.
  • Cooking: Prepare food to heal Hitpoints.
  • Woodcutting: Chop trees for logs and supplies.
  • Fletching: Create ranged weapons like bows and arrows.
  • Fishing: Catch fish for food and profit.
  • Firemaking: Light fires for experience and activities like Wintertodt.
  • Crafting: Make armor, jewelry, and more.
  • Smithing: Forge armor and weapons from ores.
  • Mining: Extract ores and minerals from rocks.
  • Herblore: Create potions for combat and skilling.
  • Agility: Speed up run energy recovery and access shortcuts.
  • Thieving: Steal items and gold from NPCs and stalls.
  • Slayer: Kill assigned monsters for huge loot and XP.
  • Farming: Grow crops, herbs, and trees for profit and resources.
  • Runecraft: Craft runes used for magic spells.
  • Hunter: Capture creatures for loot and experience.
  • Construction: Build and customize your player-owned house.
