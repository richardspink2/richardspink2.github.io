General Tips

  • Use Runelite Plugins: Aggression Timer, Ground Items, Tile Markers, Inventory Setup.
  • Organize your bank with clear tabs for faster gameplay.
  • Buy Death’s Coffer Blessings for valuable item protection on death.
  • Capitalize on daily rewards like Tears of Guthix, Battlestaves, Kingdom Management.

Skilling Tips

  • Do Birdhouse Runs every 50 minutes for passive Hunter XP and profit.
  • Plant Oak Trees and play Tithe Farm early for fast Farming XP.
  • Learn 3-Tick Fishing and Woodcutting for nearly double XP rates.
  • Wintertodt grants early cash, Firemaking XP, and valuable supplies.

Combat & Bossing Tips

  • Master Prayer Flicking to preserve prayer points during fights.
  • Choose your weapon based on DPS needs: Whip, Dagger, or Scimitar.
  • Safe spot monsters to farm Ranged/Magic XP without taking damage.
  • Use the Barrows Chest Ring of Wealth trick to boost loot chances.
  • Use Dragon Hunter Lance + Anti-Dragonfire Shield at Vorkath for easy kills.

Money-Making Tips

  • Invest in Kingdom of Miscellania for weekly passive profit.
  • Slayer training earns huge cash plus XP after level 85.
  • Flip supplies daily at the Grand Exchange for extra cash.
  • Pickpocket Master Farmers for high-value seeds after 38 Thieving.
  • Smelt bars at Blast Furnace for easy 300k+/hour profits.

Pro Secrets

  • Farm Castle Wars tickets by drawing empty world games.
  • Stack up to 5 Hard Clues for mass casket openings.
  • Save XP lamps and Tears of Guthix for slow skills like Runecrafting.
  • Unlock agility shortcuts early to speed up Slayer tasks.
  • Farm Nightmare Zone points AFK using Dharok's at low HP.

Account Optimization

  • Set up a player-owned house with teleport nexus and rejuvenation pool.
  • Complete Waterfall Quest, Grand Tree, and Priest in Peril early.
  • Combine herb runs, birdhouse runs, and battlestaves for daily profits.

RuneCraft Master Tip

"Efficiency beats effort. Plan your routes. Time your skills. Stack your dailies. And always be logged in when bonuses hit."
